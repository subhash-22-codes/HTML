Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) is an undergraduate academic degree conferred after the completion of a three or four-year program of studies at an accredited university or accredited university-level institution. B.Tech is considered one of the most prestigious and sought-after degrees in the engineering field. The program provides a strong foundation in the principles and practices of engineering, including areas such as Computer Science, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Civil Engineering, and many more. Students gain theoretical knowledge as well as practical experience through lab work, projects, and internships, preparing them for successful careers in various industries.
